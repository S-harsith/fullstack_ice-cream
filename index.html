Welcome to Moon Milk Kulfi...

Welcome to Moon Milk Kulfi, where traditional Indian frozen desserts meet the magic of starlit nights and soothing, slow-cooked goodness. Our name reflects our philosophy: we believe that a truly exceptional kulfi should feel like a comforting, celestial escape.

Inspired by the ancient wellness concept of "moon milk"—infused with calming spices, botanicals, and pure ingredients—we handcraft small-batch kulfis designed to bring pure comfort, nostalgia, and a touch of luxury to your late-night cravings and family celebrations.

Our Products...

Moon Milk Kulfi

Our homemade Moon Milk Kulfi is a celebration of time-honoured patience and pure ingredients, crafted to deliver a rich, nostalgic treat. We slow-simmer 100% fresh, full-fat milk for hours until it naturally caramelises into a dense, velvety base, entirely avoiding the artificial stabilizers, preservatives, and whipped air found in commercial ice creams. Infused with hand-ground cardamom, premium nuts, and real saffron strands, every single stick offers a luxuriously slow-melting, creamy texture that transports you straight back to the comforting flavours of traditional childhood summers.

Authentic Jigarthanda

Complementing our frozen treats is our authentic, homemade Jigarthanda, a cooling elixir meticulously prepared from scratch to live up to its name as a true 'heart cooler.' We layer this classic beverage using deeply reduced milk (bundi), natural almond gum (badam pisin), and a crowning scoop of our signature home-churned ice cream. By entirely rejecting synthetic syrups and shortcuts, we preserve the rich, refreshing heritage of Madurai’s favourite drink, offering you a deeply satisfying, all-natural refreshment that cools and soothes from the very first sip.
